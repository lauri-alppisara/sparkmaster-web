2. Purpose of the app

Sparkmaster is an offline-first maintenance companion for Yamaha and Mercury outboards. It is designed to operate primarily locally on your device. The app does not collect your personal data on our servers by default.

3. What information is processed

3.1 Data stored on your device only

The following data is stored only on your device and does not leave the app:

  • Engine details entered by you (model, serial, year, hours, naming)
  • Maintenance history you record
  • Photos you take (engine / maintenance steps)
  • Trip tracking data (GPS coordinates and time) if you use trip tracking
  • Anonymous ID (UUID) if you contribute to community features

3.2 Data shared online

If you explicitly share a contribution (e.g., part compatibility notes, tooling experience):

  • Anonymous ID (UUID)
  • The content you chose to share (text, photos, part numbers)

This is voluntary. The app does not share anything automatically.

3.3 Community licensing

Community contributions that you publish are licensed under CC-BY 4.0, meaning others may reuse them with attribution.

4. What we do not collect

The app does not collect:

  • Your name, email, or contacts (unless you add them manually in an email bug report)
  • Continuous location tracking outside trip tracking you manually start
  • Analytics or behavioural tracking
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Social network linkage

5. How data is used

Your onboard data stays under your control on-device. Sparkmaster does not upload it to Sublime Web Oy or third parties by default.

Bug reports initiated from Settings open your email client; you decide what to send.

6. Your rights

Under GDPR you may:

  • Access personal data stored on-device (everything is locally visible inside the app)
  • Erase data by uninstalling or clearing local storage / app data
  • Export summaries where Premium tools provide exports (PDF today; spreadsheets if enabled)
  • Object — simply avoid community uploads

Contact: support@sparkmaster.app

7. Security

Recommendations:

  • Protect your phone with PIN / biometric lock
  • Back up regularly
  • Keep the app updated

8. Premium & payments

Premium features are billed through Google Play. Sublime Web Oy does not process your card numbers — Google handles payment credentials.

Payments policy: https://policies.google.com/payments

9. Policy changes

We may revise this privacy policy when the product evolves. Meaningful updates will be pointed out in-app before they take effect.
